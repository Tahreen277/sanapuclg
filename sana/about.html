ABOUT OUR INSTITUTION

Sana Shaheen Independent PU College was registered in the year 2019 at Hubli. It became part of the sana educational charitable trust whose trustees comprised of members who are committed and competent visionary in the field of education.

Sana Shaheen College has always endeavoured to provide the best of resources, be it human or materia, to our students and faculty. Enrichment of student resource has been our priority and the teaching faculty is encouraged to update their technical and management skills. Improving quality is an ongoing process and We strive for excellence in all spheres.

The campus is within a five Kilometer radius of the Airport, Railway Station, Bus Stand, important places of worship, shopping malls restaurants And other civic amentities. The Institution is connected to all parts of the city through city bus and other transport facilities .
